    • ICT4M 2020

    Mental Health And Well Being In The 4th Industrial Evolution

    WHEN

    27 - 29 MARCH 2020

    WHERE

    KUANTAN,PAHANG

    ICT4M 2020 is a conference that gathers academicians, researchers,practitioners,professionals and students to address the challenges of information and Communication Technology(ICT) for the Muslim world.

    This conference has been conducted seven times so far with the most recent location in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia on 23-25 March 2020.

    In conjunction with the World Congress on Integration and Islamicization, ICT4M 2020 will be held on 27-29 March 2020 in Kuantan,Pahang.The theme for this year is "Mental Health and Well Being in the 4th Industrial Revolution."

    All accepted papers will be submitted for indexing in IEEE Xplore. Selected papers will be invited for publications in Sxopus journals.
